### Support Vector Regression (SVR)
My published research demonstrates the application of SVR to automotive engineering:

**[Support vector regression modeling of the performance of an R1234yf automotive air conditioning system](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.egypro.2018.10.010)** (2018)  
M. Suhermanto, M. S. Cakir, M. Hosoz  
*Energy Procedia 153, 309-314*  
DOI: [10.1016/j.egypro.2018.10.010](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.egypro.2018.10.010)

This work showcases:
- Implementation of SVR for performance prediction
- Model training and validation techniques
- Comparison with traditional modeling approaches
- Practical applications in automotive thermal systems
